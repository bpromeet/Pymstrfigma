## Marketing Copy Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- **Direct:** "10x better" not "potentially improved"
- **Developer-first:** Show code, not just features
- **Quantified:** "10 seconds" not "fast", "1%" not "low fees"
- **Honest:** Acknowledge stablecoin adoption challenges
- **Confident:** "Payments. But 10x Better."

### Avoid
- ‚ùå Crypto jargon (unless developer audience)
- ‚ùå Vague benefits ("better", "innovative")
- ‚ùå Overpromising (we're not "the future of money")
- ‚ùå Web3 maximalism (acknowledge Web2 advantages too)

### Use
- ‚úÖ Concrete numbers ("10s", "1%", "190+ countries")
- ‚úÖ Direct comparisons ("vs Stripe", "vs banks")
- ‚úÖ Real pain points ("7-day wait kills cash flow")
- ‚úÖ Technical accuracy (stablecoins, Account Abstraction)
